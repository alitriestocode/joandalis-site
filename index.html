About Face 21

AboutFace21 welcomes you to our website and to your first step towards achieving a more youthful, fresher appearance

On the following pages you will find details of the treatments offered at AboutFace21. Whilst many clients will choose just one treatment option, the ideal non-surgical cosmetic result will often involve a combination of treatments. Your Practitioner will be happy to offer a holistic, personalised consultation and formulate an individual treatment plan to address your needs.

Services include

  • Line relaxing injectable treatments ('botox') - Great for: lines & wrinkles caused by muscle movement eg crows feet, horizontal forehead lines, 'worry' lines between the eyebrows, 'smokers lines around the mouth, neck/jaw redefinition (the Nefertiti neck lift)
  • Dermal fillers - Great for: any facial lines caused by loss of collagen eg 'lines and wrinkles in the lower face, 'smokers' lines, nose to mouth lines and folds, turning up mouth corners (no more looking miserable even when you're feeling fine!), reshaping cheeks/lips/noses/chins
  • Obagi skin system (see www.obagi.com) - This system is amazing! its the only system I have found so far that actually works and skin improvement is often visible within weeks! Great for: sun damaged skin, finer lines and wrinkles, open pores, restoring skin radiance
  • Medical strength chemical peels Great for: Brightening & tightening the skin, fine lines & wrinkels, acne scarring, pigmentation Medical needling (see www.genuinedermaroller.com Great for : Stimulating collagen production therefore tightening and firming the skin, crepey skin, fine lines & wrinkles, stretch marks)
